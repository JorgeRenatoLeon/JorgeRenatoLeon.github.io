DPP Interoperability Vocabulary and JSON-LD Contexts

This site hosts the vocabulary definitions and JSON-LD context files for the Digital Product Passport (DPP) Interoperability Platform, developed as part of a Design Science Research thesis at Vrije Universiteit Amsterdam.

Vocabularies

RDF vocabulary files defining the classes and properties used by the Shared Core Schema and its extensions.
